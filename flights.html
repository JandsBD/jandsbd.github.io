Flights & Travel

Flight Options:

London to Verona:

RyanAir: Stanstead -> Verona

EasyJet: Gatwick -> Verona

British Airways: Gatwick -> Verona

London to Milan:

Flying from London to Milan and then getting the train is also an option, however then you would have to hire a car and drive to Verona, or get the train from Milan to Verona.

The train timetables for May aren't available yet, but you can either book tickets through ItaliaRail or Trainline.
