Gifts

In Easten Europe (we're from Moldova and almost Serbia if you didn't know) itâ€™s a wedding tradition for guests to give money as a gift to the newlyweds. We're following the Moldovan tradition (because who doesn't love money) and we decided that there will be no gift registry.

During the typical Moldovan wedding, either on the Bride and Groom's table or it's own special table, lives a cute lil wooden box made to look like a house. During the speeches, the box will be taken around the room by the God Parents of the couple (In Moldova, there's God Parents for the couple, and the kids, but they're chosen seperately. God Parents in this sense are the "guardians" of the Bride and Groom).

Envelopes will be available for those wishing to contribute, there is no set amount and definitely no limit, just however much you want to give.
